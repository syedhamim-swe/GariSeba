Streamlining Vehicle Services in Bangladesh

This dashboard provides an interactive overview of the GariSeba capstone project, outlining its objectives, proposed solution, development plan, and team structure to address the fragmented vehicle service market in Bangladesh.

Project Abstract

GariSeba is a comprehensive digital platform designed to centralize and streamline vehicle services in Bangladesh. It addresses market fragmentation, lack of transparency, and inconvenience for vehicle owners. By offering online service booking, a vehicle marketplace, and digital document management, GariSeba aims to enhance user convenience, empower service providers, and foster trust within the automotive ecosystem.

Core Purpose

The primary goal is to transform the current opaque and inefficient vehicle service landscape into an integrated, transparent, and user-friendly digital ecosystem. GariSeba connects vehicle owners with verified service providers, simplifies administrative tasks, and builds a foundation of trust through transparent ratings and secure transactions.

Project Objectives

Enhance Convenience

Achieve 85% user satisfaction in UAT by providing an effortless service booking and management interface.

Foster Trust

Implement a reliable rating system, targeting 60% of completed services to receive a user review.

Ensure Scalability

Design a backend architecture to support 1,000 concurrent users and pass all security assessments.

Addressing Market Challenges

GariSeba is designed to directly solve critical pain points in Bangladesh's vehicle service industry. Hover over each problem to see how our platform provides a targeted solution.

Service Fragmentation

Owners struggle to find reliable mechanics, garages, and parts shops from a sea of isolated providers.

A unified platform with searchable, filterable listings of verified service providers, consolidating the market.

Lack of Transparency

Inconsistent pricing, hidden fees, and uncertainty about service quality erode consumer trust.

Implement transparent pricing, user ratings, and a verifiable review system to build accountability.

Inconvenience

Manual booking processes, physical visits, and complex paperwork are time-consuming and inefficient.

Streamlined online booking, real-time scheduling, and digital document management with automated reminders.
